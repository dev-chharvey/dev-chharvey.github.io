Learning Objectives

Sequences

  • Given a recursive or explicit formula for a sequence, write the first few terms of the sequence or find a specific term.
  • Given a sequence of terms, write a rule that describes the sequence.
  • Calculate arithmetic and geometric means between two numbers.

Series

  • Find the sum of the first few terms in a series.
  • Determine whether an infinite series converges or diverges.
  • Find the infinite sum of a convergent series.
