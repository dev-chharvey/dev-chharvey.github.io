Quiz 1.4: Order of Operations

Use a No. 2 Pencil. Show all work.

#1–3: Evaluate the following expressions.

  1. 100-40\times2

  2. \left(2+10\right)\div\left(5-1\right)+2

  3. \frac{136+8^2}{2^2}+40

  1. Betty's work is shown below. If she made a mistake, explain what she did wrong and correct her work. If there are no mistakes, write no errors.

    \left(3+4\right)^2\\ 3^2+4^2\\ 9+16\\ 25

  2. Create an expression using only the numbers 1, 2, 3, and 4; three operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, or exponents); and one set of meaningful parentheses. Then, evaluate your expression. (Extra credit if you can make your expression equal 40.)
