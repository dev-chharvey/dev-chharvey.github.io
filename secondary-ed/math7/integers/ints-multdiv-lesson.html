Lesson 1.3: Integer Multiplication and Division

SOL
7.3a The student will model addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division of integers.
7.3b The student will add, subtract, multiply, and divide integers.
Timeframe
Week I.5
Time required
3 days
Lesson created
Sep 24, 2013
Lesson updated
Sep 24, 2013

Preparation

Objectives

  • The student will model multiplication and division of integers (with the same sign and different signs) using two-colored counters, a two-sided mat, and a number line.
  • The student will evaluate the product and quotient of two integers, with the same sign or different signs.

Essential Questions

  • How do I multiply integers?
  • Why does (-1)(-1)=1?

Vocabulary

product
the answer to a multiplication problem
quotient
the answer to a division problem
