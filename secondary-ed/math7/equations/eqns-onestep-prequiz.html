#12–16: Solve each word problem and answer in complete sentences.

  1. Garrett's business spent 7 million dollars in the first three months of the year. In the first six months total, his business spent 15 million dollars. How much money did his business spend in the second three months?

  2. Gina owed her mom $30 on Monday. On Tuesday, she paid some money back and now she only owes $18. How much money did she pay her mom?

  3. Gus was having a party for 12 people. For the party, he ordered 36 subs from the local deli. Each sub was 6 inches long. How many subs did he expect each person to eat?

  4. Ms. Brunelle made a number of copies of the Benchmark Exam for all the 7th Grade math students. She distributed them evenly to all three 7th Grade math teachers. Assuming each teacher had 60 students, how many copies did Ms. Brunelle make?

  5. Extra credit: A square playground has an area of 400 square meters. How many meters long is each side of the playground?

    More extra credit: Say you want to buy a chain-link fence to surround the playground on all four sides. What is the perimeter of the playground?
