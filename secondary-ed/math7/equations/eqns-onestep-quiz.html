Math 7 — Mr. Harvey

Quiz 2.2: One-Step Equations

Use a No. 2 Pencil. Show all work.

#1–5: Solve each equation.

  1. x+4=-8
  2. 3x=-81
  3. \frac{x}{11}=-16
  4. \frac{2}{3}x=-12
  5. Extra credit: x^3=64

#6–8: Set up an equation, with a variable, for each word problem below. Then solve the problem and answer in complete sentences.

  1. Gina owed her mom $30 on Monday. On Tuesday, she paid some money back and now she only owes $18. How much money did she pay her mom?

  2. Gus went to the store and bought 5 sweatshirts. He spent a total of $45. What was the price of each shirt?

  3. Extra credit: A square playground has an area of 400 square meters. How many meters long is each side of the playground?

    More extra credit: Say you want to buy a chain-link fence to surround the playground on all four sides. What is the perimeter of the playground?
