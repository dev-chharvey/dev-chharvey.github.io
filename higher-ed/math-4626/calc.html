Calculating Limits

Site Map

There are three main methods of determining the limit of a function: by using a table of values to estimate the limit, viewing the graph of a function to conjecture the limit, or working with equations to explicitly computing the limit. This page takes you through the steps of the first two methods. Go to the Analytical Methods page for the third method.
