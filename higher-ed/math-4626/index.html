Goal

To provide a tool for students and/or teachers to deeply investigate an important why question, while connecting topics across the secondary school curriculum and college-level mathematics.

Task

Investigate one of the why questions touched upon in class and posted on Blackboard. Your project should make use of technology and existing web resources. You will share the final product on the Internet through media such as YouTube, Wikipedia, The Math Forum, a blog, a math education listserv, or an online journal. Whatever forum you choose, make sure you understand your intended audience: secondary school teachers and/or students.

Make sure you build from what is already out there on the Internet so that you are not just re-inventing the wheel, so to speak. This means your project should begin with a thorough search of various media on the Internet. You may want to hyperlink or otherwise refer to existing products to enhance your own new product.

Grading Rubric

Mathematical Depth

The product should entail deep mathematical insights that make connections to college-level mathematical topics.

Pedagogy

The product should clearly target an appropriate audience and explicitly build from what that audience should understand already, motivating the audience to engage in mathematical thinking.

Utility

The product should be easy to use and likely will be used by teachers and/or students.
